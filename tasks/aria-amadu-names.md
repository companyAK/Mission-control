# Business Names for Amadu Kamara's Web + Ads Agency

Created: 2026-03-03  
Purpose: Service business growth agency (websites + ads)

---

## 1. **Kamara Scale**

**Why it works:**  
"Scale" is exactly what service businesses want — growth, expansion, more customers. Kamara feels strong and memorable. The name is direct about what you deliver.

**Personal brand angle:**  
Positions Amadu as the architect of growth. "Kamara" becomes synonymous with scaling. When clients succeed, they'll say "We scaled with Kamara."

**Tagline:**  
*"Where Service Businesses Grow"*

**Memorability: 9/10**  
Two syllables, clear meaning, easy to spell. Strong verb association.

---

## 2. **Amadu Launch**

**Why it works:**  
"Launch" signals fresh starts and momentum — perfect for businesses that need visibility. Action-oriented, optimistic, modern. Sounds like a tech company but accessible.

**Personal brand angle:**  
AK becomes "the launch guy" — the person who takes businesses from invisible to visible. Every new client is a launch event.

**Tagline:**  
*"From Hidden to Seen"*

**Memorability: 8/10**  
Easy to say, memorable imagery. "Launch" creates a vivid mental picture.

## 3. **AK Studio**

**Why it works:**  
Clean, modern, professional. "Studio" suggests craftsmanship and creativity without being pretentious. AK initials feel personal but not overly casual.

**Personal brand angle:**  
The studio model positions AK as a master craftsman with a workshop of tools (agents, design, ads). Clients come to the studio to build something great.

**Tagline:**  
*"Built Right. Seen Everywhere."*

**Memorability: 7/10**  
Simple and clean, though "studio" is common. The AK makes it unique.

AK provides the lift that struggling businesses need. Not just maintenance — actual elevation. Every project lifts the client higher.

**Tagline:**  
*"Elevate Your Business"*

**Memorability: 8/10**
