## Research Methodology & Notes

### Struggling Business Indicators (used for profiling)
1. **Google ratings 2.5-3.5 stars** with LOW review volume (<30)
2. **No website** or severely outdated (pre-2019 design, broken mobile)
3. **Dormant social media** (last post >6 months ago)
4. **No review engagement** (never responds to Google/Yelp reviews)
5. **Phone-only operations** in 2026 = missed opportunities

### Austin Market Context
- **High-growth suburbs:** Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Buda/Kyle
- **Service density:** Oversaturated in central Austin, gaps in suburbs
- **Competition:** National chains + app platforms squeezing independents
- **Opportunity:** Established locals with quality + modern marketing = competitive advantage
