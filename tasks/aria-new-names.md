## Domain Verification Next Steps

Before finalizing any name:
1. Check domain availability (.com priority)
2. Search USPTO trademark database
3. Google the exact name + "agency" or "marketing"
4. Check social media handle availability
5. Review state business registrations

**Note:** These names avoid generic terms (Digital, Solutions, Agency, Pro), geographic ties, and known competitors (LeadLift, RiseLocal). All are designed to be nationally scalable and speak directly to service business growth.
