## 🎯 RECOMMENDED RESEARCH METHODS

### Method 1: Google Maps (Most Effective)
**Search Terms:**
- "cleaning service Austin TX"
- "landscaping Austin TX"  
- "lawn care Cedar Park TX" (Austin suburb)
- "house cleaning Round Rock TX" (Austin suburb)

**Filter Strategy:**
1. Sort by **rating** (find 1-3 star businesses)
2. Click on each result and check:
   - Review count (low = struggling with visibility)
   - Last review date (>6 months = declining)
   - Website quality (none/broken = need help)
   - Social media presence (none = missed opportunities)

### Method 2: Yelp Austin
- Search "cleaning service" + "Austin"
- Filter: 1-3 stars
- Look for: No website listed, old photos, generic descriptions

### Method 3: Facebook Local Search
- Search "Cleaning service Austin Texas"
- Look for pages with:
  - <100 followers
  - Irregular posting (last post >3 months ago)
  - No website link in bio

### Method 4: Nextdoor (Goldmine for Local Services)
- Browse "Services" category
- Find providers with:
  - Few recommendations
  - Generic listings
  - No professional branding

## 💡 QUALIFICATION CRITERIA (Use This When Evaluating)

### ✅ GOOD FIT SIGNALS
- 1-3 stars with 5-20 reviews
- Website is missing, broken, or clearly DIY from 2015
- Owner/operator business (not franchise)
- Facebook/Instagram exists but inactive (3+ months)
- Responsive to phone calls (shows they want business)

### ❌ AVOID
- 0 stars / 0 reviews (brand new or dead)
- Franchises (corporate marketing handles it)
- 4+ stars with professional site (already figured it out)
- No contact info / permanently closed
- Already using pro marketing services
