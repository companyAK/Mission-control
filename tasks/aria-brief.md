# Creative Brief: Agency Business Names

## Task
Generate 8-10 business name options for AK's web + ads agency.

## Business Profile
- **What we do:** Help small service businesses get more customers through websites + ads
- **Target Market:** Service businesses (plumbers, electricians, HVAC, cleaners, landscapers, laundry, handymen)
- **Location:** Austin, TX metro (but scalable nationally)
- **Client Pain:** Low reviews, no web presence, struggling visibility
- **Our Solve:** Simple, approachable, no-nonsense website + ad management

## Name Criteria
1. **Approachable** — Not corporate/intimidating. Feels like a real person/team who "gets it"
2. **Clarity** — Immediately obvious what we do (web + ads, business growth)
3. **Memorable** — Easy to say, spell, remember. Referral-friendly.
4. **Local Credibility** — Austin connection is nice but not required (can scale nationally)
5. **Ownable** — Domain available (.com preferred), social handles open
6. **Tone:** Professional but friendly, helpful, growth-focused

## Deliverables
Provide 8-10 name options with:
1. Name
2. Why it works (positioning)
3. Domain availability estimate (likely available? taken?)
4. Short 1-liner tagline (optional but helps)

Example format:
```
**TrafficPulse**
Why: Traffic = customers, Pulse = growth/energy. Clear + memorable.
Domain: Likely available (common word combo).
Tagline: "Get more customers, period."
```

## Tone Notes
- Austin local would be bonus (but not required)
- Avoid: Generic buzzwords (Digital, Solutions, Agency, Pro, etc.)
- Prefer: Action words (Build, Grow, Drive, Traffic, Rise, etc.)
