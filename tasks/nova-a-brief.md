# Research Brief: Austin Trades (NOVA-A)

## Task
Find 3-4 struggling plumbers, electricians, HVAC companies in Austin metro that are good prospects for AK's web/ad agency.

## Search Criteria
- **Service Types:** Plumbers, electricians, HVAC contractors
- **"Struggling" Signals:** 3 stars or lower reviews OR no website OR outdated website OR low social media
- **Geography:** Austin metro + surrounding areas
- **Contact:** Phone, email, website status

## Deliverables (per prospect)
1. Company name, service type, location
2. Phone, website (if exists)
3. Google rating + review count
4. Social media status (active? inactive?)
5. Why they're a prospect (which pain points strongest?)
