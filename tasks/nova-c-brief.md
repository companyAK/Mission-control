# Research Brief: Austin Service & Specialty (NOVA-C)

## Task
Find 3-4 struggling laundry delivery, handymen, or other service businesses in Austin metro.

## Search Criteria
- **Service Types:** Laundry delivery, handymen, and similar service trades
- **"Struggling" Signals:** 3 stars or lower OR no website OR outdated website OR low social media
- **Geography:** Austin metro + surrounding areas
- **Contact:** Phone, email, website status

## Deliverables (per prospect)
1. Company name, service type, location
2. Phone, website (if exists)
3. Google rating + review count
4. Social media status
5. Why they're a prospect
