## 5. AK's Pricing Model Evaluation

### Proposed Pricing:
- **Website Creation:** $400-500 (one-time)
- **Monthly Hosting:** $100/month

### Competitive Analysis:

#### ✅ **Strengths**

1. **Highly Affordable Entry Point**
   - $400-500 is 50-80% cheaper than typical agency pricing
   - Accessible to low-review service businesses with tight budgets
   - Easy "yes" for businesses that need online presence NOW

2. **Predictable Recurring Revenue**
   - $100/month × 12 = $1,200/year per client
   - 10 clients = $12,000/year recurring
   - 25 clients = $30,000/year recurring

3. **Massive Profit Margins on Hosting**
   - Cost: $4.66/month per client (or as low as $0.36 when scaled)
   - Revenue: $100/month per client
   - **Gross Margin: 95.3%** (at individual level)
   - Even accounting for time/support, this is highly profitable

4. **Referral-Friendly Positioning**
   - Low price + good service = strong word-of-mouth
   - Service businesses talk to each other (contractors, trade groups)
   - "My web guy is great and affordable" spreads fast

5. **Competitive vs. Austin Market**
   - Undercuts most freelancers on creation cost
   - Monthly fee is in the "sweet spot" ($75-150 range)
   - Provides managed service without sticker shock

#### ⚠️ **Potential Concerns**

1. **Perceived Value Risk**
   - $400 might seem "too cheap" to some prospects (quality concerns)
   - **Solution:** Emphasize speed, quality portfolio, testimonials

2. **Scope Creep Risk**
   - At $400-500, clients might expect unlimited revisions
   - **Solution:** Clear contract with defined deliverables (e.g., "5 pages, 2 revision rounds")

3. **Monthly Churn Risk**
   - Clients might ask "what am I paying $100/month for?"
   - **Solution:** Bundle hosting with ongoing value:
     - Monthly uptime monitoring
     - Security updates
     - Email support (quick response)
     - Minor content updates (1-2/month)
     - Performance optimization
     - Backup management

4. **Competition from DIY Platforms**
   - Wix ($16/month), Squarespace ($23/month) offer cheaper hosting
   - **Solution:** Position as "done-for-you" + support + speed + local expertise
