## 📋 Recommended Manual Research Process

To find struggling trades companies, use this workflow:

### Step 1: Google Maps Search
1. Search: `plumbers near Austin, TX`
2. Filter: **Sort by rating** (ascending if possible, or manually scan)
3. Look for: **2.0-3.5 stars with 10+ reviews**
4. Red flags: "Unprofessional," "didn't show up," "overcharged"

### Step 2: Website Assessment
For each prospect:
- Visit their website (if listed)
- Check for: Outdated design, broken links, no mobile optimization, slow load times
- Note: Many won't have a website at all = prime opportunity

### Step 3: Social Media Check
- Search: `[Business Name] Facebook/Instagram`
- Red flags: Last post >6 months ago, <100 followers, no engagement

### Step 4: Contact Verification
- Call the number to verify business is still operating
- Note if voicemail sounds professional or homemade
