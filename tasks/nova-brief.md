# Research Brief: Austin Metro Service Businesses

## Task
Find 10 struggling service businesses in Austin metro + surrounding areas that are good prospects for AK's web/ad agency.

## Search Criteria

### Service Types (Priority)
- Trades (plumbers, electricians, HVAC)
- Cleaners (house cleaning, commercial)
- Landscaping & lawn care
- Laundry delivery services
- Handymen

### "Struggling" Signals (ALL combined)
1. **Google Reviews:** 3 stars or lower (or very few reviews)
2. **Website:** No website OR outdated/broken website
3. **Social Media:** No activity OR inactive/abandoned accounts
4. **Overall:** Clearly need help with visibility

### Geography
- Austin metro (Austin proper, surrounding suburbs)
- Areas around Austin (willing to expand radius)

## Deliverables

For each of the 10 prospects, provide:
1. Company name
2. Service type
3. Location/address
4. Phone number
5. Website status (has one? working? outdated?)
6. Google review count + rating
7. Social media presence (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn — active or inactive?)
8. Why they're a good prospect (which signals are strongest?)
9. Estimated outreach angle (what's their main pain point?)

## Format
Save to: /root/.openclaw/workspace/tasks/nova-output.md

## Priority
Start with 3-star reviews first, then expand to no-reviews/outdated-website as you find them.

Quality > Speed. Verify contact info works.
